Shipping charges should be configurable from the admin/backend panel.



When a customer changes their delivery location during checkout, the shipping charge should be recalculated automatically based on the selected location.



Suggested approach for easy management:



* Create Shipping Zones (e.g., Dhaka City, Dhaka Outside City, Chattogram, Other Districts, etc.).

* Each zone will have its own configurable shipping charge in the admin panel.

* Admins can add, update, or disable shipping zones without requiring code changes.

* During checkout, customers select their district/area, and the system automatically applies the corresponding shipping charge.

* The order summary should update instantly to reflect the new shipping cost and grand total.

* Shipping charges applied to an order should be stored with the order to ensure historical accuracy, even if shipping rates change later.

* The system should support free shipping rules, such as free delivery for orders above a configurable minimum amount.

* A default shipping charge should be available as a fallback for locations that are not assigned to any shipping zone.
